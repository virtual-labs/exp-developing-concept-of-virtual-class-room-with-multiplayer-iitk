A smart classroom integrates digital technology into the learning environment to create an
interactive and engaging educational experience. In this virtual lab, multiple display systems are
used to present questions and activities to participants. Learners interact with the system using
controllers or input devices, enabling real-time participation and assessment. <br><br>
This setup promotes active learning by encouraging students to respond to questions, test their
knowledge, and receive immediate feedback. The virtual environment eliminates physical
constraints, allowing participants to collaborate and practice in a simulated classroom regardless
of location. Such a system enhances understanding, retention, and problem-solving skills while
making the learning process more dynamic and enjoyable.
