1: About

1.1: Models 
This is (as promised in our 3d fan pack asset) an asset pack of ceiling fans! There are 15 ceiling fans ready for use in games/apps. The fan has been highly optimized but there's always room for customization (editing). Read the "Materials" and "Extras" section on how to customize the ceiling fan to best fit your project!

1.2: Materials
Materials have been renamed (thanks to your feedbacks) for easy customization. You can easily edit the materials to change the look of the model, or change the model look entirely by editing the source file included in the source folder (Model > Source). 

1.3: Extras
For additional support regarding the asset, kindly check the description. The .blend file has been included for easy and additional customization. Please note, you also get an additional request of a ceiling fan model of your choice!
